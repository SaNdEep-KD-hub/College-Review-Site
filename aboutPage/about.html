About us

This website is designed to offer specific information to the students who are interested for the UG/PG courses and colleges providing the courses across the country on the most popular educational streams like - Management; Science & Engineering; Banking & Finance; Information Technology;Hospitality,Arts, Law. We have several student oriented tools like, College Compare, College Reviews, MyShortlist and Ask and Answer. We also provide information about the previous year cut off, fees of each college so that students can decide to which college they should go for to make their career vitally. college reviews are a critical part of our website. Students share their college experiences on this platform, enabling education seekers to make an informed choice about their academic journey. As choosing a right course and educational institute providing that course within a student's budget from where they can learn the skills and can occupy a job is a very confusing and crusial process our website is there standing beside the students to help them making the right decision at right time to choose the course and college according to their requierment and expectation. Students can access college placement statistics, academic and infrastructure standards through these reviews which will help them not only choosing a good career but also help them to influence others to make their career. Our ultimate goal is to remove the confussions of struggling students by giving them clarified facts and reviews so that they can build their career happiely.
